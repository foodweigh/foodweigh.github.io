About FoodWeigh

FoodWeigh is a mobile application designed to help users track the weight of their meals. By weighing each meal, users can better understand their daily food intake and view calculated totals for food consumed throughout the day.

The app focuses on simplicity, accuracy, and consistency to support users who want better awareness of their eating habits.
